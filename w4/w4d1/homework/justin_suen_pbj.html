Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • A heap of peanut butter
  • A heap of any jelly

Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches are an American staple.

Directions

  1. Put the two slices of bread on a flat surface.
  2. Get a knife and spread the peanut butter on one bread.
  3. Clean off the knife and spread the jelly on the other bread.
  4. Combine the bread with the sauces touching each other.

This is an original recipe.
